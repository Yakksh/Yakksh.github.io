About Me

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Rajasthan Institute of Engineering & Technology, with a CGPA of 9.2. My passion lies in AI, data science, and software development, where I enjoy transforming data into actionable insights.

I have a solid technical foundation in Python, SQL, JavaScript, and frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, React, and Django. My experience spans building APIs, web scraping, sentiment analysis, and creating machine learning forecasting models.

At FormulaQ Solutions, as well as during my time with DataWalk.in and DeepThought EduTech Ventures, I focused on delivering data-driven solutions that enhance business outcomes. Whether it's developing backend systems or analyzing data to guide strategy, I thrive on solving complex problems.

Fluent in English and Hindi, I enjoy working in diverse, collaborative teams. I'm eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities in AI, machine learning, and software development.
